Our Story

Every Client Is a Gem

At Touched By A Jewell, we believe your hair is your crowning jewel — and it deserves to be treated as such.

Nestled in the heart of South Orange, NJ, our salon was built on one core belief: every person who walks through our doors is precious. Like a skilled jeweler who handles only the finest stones, we approach every head of hair with intention, artistry, and care.

We specialize in the full spectrum of hair services — from silk presses that gleam like polished gold, to color transformations that shimmer with depth, to protective styles that honor your natural texture. Whether you’re a new guest or a returning gem in our collection, you’ll always receive our most devoted, personal attention.
